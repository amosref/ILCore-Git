0. Extension
Definition

Extension: Immigration information for patient - for the purpose of this extension - immigration is an international movement of a person to a destination country in order to settle as permanent resident

ShortExt: Patient Immigration
Comments

In case of an Aliyah (A jew immigrating to Israel), the destination should be 'IL' (Israel)
