Expansion based on codesystem IL Core Not Perfomed Reasons v0.13.1 (CodeSystem)

This value set contains 4 concepts

CodeSystemDisplayDefinition
  not-performed-medical-reasonhttp://fhir.health.gov.il/cs/il-core-not-performed-reasonnot performed medical reason

The value is not available because the observation procedure was not performed due to Medical Reason

  not-performed-administrative-reasonhttp://fhir.health.gov.il/cs/il-core-not-performed-reasonnot performed administrative reason

The value is not available because the observation procedure was not performed due to Administrative Reason, e.g., observation cannot be measured because of a remote visit (telemedicine), absent practitioner (patient need EKG but no technician available), etc.

  not-performed-equipment-reasonhttp://fhir.health.gov.il/cs/il-core-not-performed-reasonnot performed equipment reason

The value is not available because the observation procedure was not performed due to Equipment Reason, e.g., EKG malfunction, blood pressure monitor out of batteries, autoscope only available in adult size and the patient is two years old, etc

  not-performed-patient-reasonhttp://fhir.health.gov.il/cs/il-core-not-performed-reasonnot performed patient reason

The value is not available because the observation procedure was not performed due to Patient Reason e.g., patient refused to do the test, patient didn’t come etc.
